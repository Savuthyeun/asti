Where We Work

ASTI operates in three countries plus regional ASEAN — each with tailored partnerships, activities, and results.

Cambodia

ASTI works closely with DGTVET under the Ministry of Labor and Vocational Training, alongside business chambers CAMFEBA and IBC, to design, deliver, and certify WBL programmes across targeted industrial sectors in Cambodia.
